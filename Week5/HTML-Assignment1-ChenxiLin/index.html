NBA Finals

NBA Finals is almost here and there's a lot of hype this time, critiques are saying this years playoffs is the best compared to the previous few playoff years. Which I totally agree with. This years playoff is unpredictable in the sense that the main teams that was favored all went out in the semifinals.

Right now, the final is only about a week away and the teams that are competing in the conference finals are Indiana paces and the New York Knick. They are battling it out to see who goes on top, right now the Pacers are ahead by one. If the Pacers win this next game they will go up to compete again the Oklahoma Thunders.

The Oklahoma thunders will be in the finals the second time since 2012, they have a great team this season with there main star player Shai Gilgeous-Alexander taking lead of the team. There roster is stacked with really good players like Chet Holmgren, Jalen Williams, Alex Caruso. Who ever is there competition will have trouble.
